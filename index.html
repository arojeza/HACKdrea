Tiramisu Recipe

A delightful Italian dessert made simple.

Welcome to my famous tiramisu recipe. My name is Andrea and I hope you get to share this delicious dessert with everyone. Let's get started.

This is how you tiramisu should look like!

Ingredients

  • 6 egg yolks
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 1/4 cup mascarpone cheese
  • 1 3/4 cup heavy cream
  • 2 cups brewed espresso, cooled
  • 1 pack of ladyfingers
  • Cocoa powder for dusting

Instructions

  1. Whisk egg yolks and sugar until creamy.
  2. Fold in mascarpone cheese until smooth.
  3. Whip heavy cream to stiff peaks and fold into the mixture.
  4. Dip ladyfingers quickly in espresso and layer them in a dish.
  5. Spread the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers.
  6. Repeat layers and finish with mascarpone mixture.
  7. Dust with cocoa powder and refrigerate for 4 hours before serving.
